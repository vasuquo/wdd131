Easy Shopping Site Plan

Site Name

Easy Shopping Mall

This name represents a company providing online shopping facilities, giving clients means to shop anywhere in the world that they are located.
Domain availability: easyshop.ng.

Site Purpose

The site provides a platform for online shoppers to buy goods and have them delivered to them anywhere they want. The home page introduces the various types of products available. The products page provides more details on products offers facilities select, sort and categorize products. The cart page allows clients to add purchased items to an electronic cart, computes total purchases. The checkout page for packaging purchased items for onward delivery to respective destinations. The accounts page allows clients to register or login and make enquiries.
